About Me

My name is Ammar Siddiqi and I am a junior at Texas A&M, studying Computer Engineering. I am currently working a part-time job as a student technician for the college of Veterinary Medicine at my university, and I am expected to graduate in May 2022.

I was raised in the Illinois suburbs. I came to Texas over the 2018 summer, and I have not missed the North too much (except during the peak summers, it's too hot here). I chose to attend Texas A&M University because of its welcoming community, its massively resourced program for engineers, and its excellent alumni association. I went for engineering because my father was an electrical engineer and I wanted to follow under his footsteps. Also because I enjoy solving difficult problems and learning more about technology.

Shoot me an e-mail or check out my LinkedIn or Github if you'd like to know more about me or my work.
